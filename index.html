The "what's the catch?" section.

So it's actually free?

Yes. We design, build, and install the booth into your wall at no cost to the venue, and we maintain it. You provide the wall and the guests.

How much space does it need?

A single open wall is enough. Because it's recessed and mirror-format, it takes up zero floor space — our contractor confirms placement during the free site visit.

Who controls the branding?

You do. Every strip and GIF carries your logo and venue details. We set it up to match your look and can refresh it for events.

What happens with the guest emails?

Guests opt in at the booth. We put the list to work for you — weekly emails with your specials, promos for monthly events, and slow-night offers that bring guests back. You set it all in your client portal; we handle the rest.

What does my venue need to qualify?

Consistent foot traffic and a wall to build into. Bars are our sweet spot, but any busy venue can apply below.
